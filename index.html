About Us

Coding is increasingly recognized as a vital skill, enabling individuals to interact with technology while fostering essential abilities like problem-solving, creativity, and critical thinking. These skills are applicable across various industries, making coding education crucial for future career success. Python, one of the most commonly taught programming languages in schools, is particularly favored due to its ease of learning and versatility.

At FEU Alabang, students' proficiency in Python is evaluated through discussions, hands-on projects, and exams. However, challenges exist; weekly classes may hinder engagement and retention of information. While hands-on projects enhance learning, they may not fully assess students' understanding of coding concepts. Additionally, exams often prioritize memorization over practical application of knowledge.

The ultimate goal is to create a motivating tool that enhances students' learning experiences in Python programming.
